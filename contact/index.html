Before You Contact Us

You might find an answer to your question right here. These are the most common inquiries we receive:

"Is this calculator accurate?"
Yes. Our calculator uses standard financial formulas as defined by Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP). The Basic EPS, Diluted EPS, P/E Ratio, and EPS Growth calculations follow the same methodology used by financial analysts worldwide. All calculations happen in your browser using straightforward arithmetic, so the math is transparent and verifiable. However, the accuracy of your results depends on the accuracy of the data you input. Always verify your inputs against official company filings.

"Can I use this calculator on my website?"
Linking to EPS Calculator is always welcome, and we appreciate it when you share our tool with others. If you'd like to embed the calculator directly on your website, please contact us first to discuss permission and proper attribution. We're generally happy to work with educational institutions and non-commercial projects.

"I found a bug"
Thank you for helping us improve! When reporting a bug, please include as much detail as possible so we can reproduce and fix the issue:

  • Which browser are you using? (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge, etc.)
  • What device? (Desktop, iPhone, Android tablet, etc.)
  • What steps led to the problem?
  • What did you expect to happen vs. what actually happened?
  • Screenshots are extremely helpful if possible

"I have a feature request"
We welcome suggestions for new features, additional calculator tabs, or improvements to existing functionality. Some of our best ideas have come from user feedback. Let us know what would make this tool more useful for you, whether it's a new metric to calculate, a different way to display results, or additional educational content.
