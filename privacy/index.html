Privacy Policy

Summary (Plain English)

We respect your privacy and have designed EPS Calculator with privacy in mind. Here's what you need to know:

  • We don't collect your personal data. No account is required to use our calculators.
  • All calculations happen in your browser. The numbers you enter never leave your device.
  • Nothing is stored on our servers. When you close the page, your data is gone.
  • We don't track your calculations. We have no record of what you calculate or the results.

The rest of this policy provides more detail, but that's the essence of it.

Information We Don't Collect

EPS Calculator is designed to be a simple, privacy-respecting tool. We specifically do not collect:

  • Account information: No registration, login, or account is required. We don't ask for your name, email (except via the optional contact form), password, or any other account details.
  • Financial data: The earnings figures, stock prices, share counts, and other financial data you enter into our calculators are processed entirely in your web browser using JavaScript. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
  • Calculation history: We don't store or log your calculation inputs or results. Each session is independent, and nothing persists after you leave the page.
  • Personal identifiers: We don't collect or store personal identifiers like your name, address, phone number, or social security number.

Information That May Be Collected

While we minimize data collection, some information may be collected through standard web technologies:

Analytics: We may use basic analytics to understand how our site is used in aggregate. This may include anonymous data such as page views, general geographic region (country level), device type (mobile vs. desktop), and browser type. This data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify individual users.

Server Logs: Our hosting provider (Vercel) may maintain standard server logs that include IP addresses, timestamps, and pages requested. These logs are used for security monitoring and troubleshooting and are typically retained for a limited period.

Cookies: We use minimal cookies. Essential cookies may be used for basic site functionality. We do not use cookies for advertising or cross-site tracking. See the Cookies section below for more detail.

Contact Data

If you choose to contact us via email, the following applies:

  • The information you provide (email address and message content) is used solely to respond to your inquiry.
  • We do not share your contact information with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Contact data is retained only as long as necessary to address your inquiry and for our records.
  • You may request deletion of your contact data at any time by emailing us.

Third-Party Services

EPS Calculator uses the following third-party services:

  • Vercel: Our website is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may collect standard server logs as described above. Vercel's privacy policy applies to their infrastructure services.

We do not use advertising networks, retargeting services, or social media tracking pixels.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. Here's how we use them:

  • Essential Cookies: We may use minimal essential cookies required for basic site functionality. These do not track you across sites.
  • Analytics Cookies: If we use analytics, these cookies help us understand site usage in aggregate. They do not identify you personally.
  • No Advertising Cookies: We do not use cookies for advertising, remarketing, or cross-site tracking.

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically delete existing cookies or configure your browser to reject cookies, though this may affect site functionality.

Your Rights

You have rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Since we don't collect personal data from calculator usage, there is no personal data to access.
  • Deletion: If you have contacted us via the contact form and wish to have that data deleted, you can request deletion by emailing us at info@epscalculator.org.
  • Correction: If you believe any information we hold about you is inaccurate, contact us and we will correct it.

Children's Privacy

EPS Calculator is a general-purpose financial education tool. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information through the contact form, please contact us and we will delete that information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of EPS Calculator after changes to this policy constitutes acceptance of the updated terms.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: info@epscalculator.org

We will respond to privacy-related inquiries within a reasonable timeframe.
